---

### Blessed Rounds (Special Ammo)

**Role:** Bonus damage vs eldritch enemies  
**Best For:** All classes (load into any gun)  
**Cost:** 5,000 scrip + 5 boss kills (one-time unlock)

| Stat           | Effect                                             |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Damage Bonus   | +50% damage vs eldritch enemies (Tier 2-4 enemies) |
| Damage Penalty | -25% damage vs corrupted humans (Tier 1 enemies)   |
| Ammo Type      | Can load into ANY gun                              |
| Magazine       | Uses normal gun magazine size                      |
| Availability   | Limited drops in high-saturation areas             |

**How It Works:**

- Craft at hub: 100 scrip per magazine
- Find in world: Rare drops (10% chance) from boss kills
- Load like normal ammo, but glows golden
- Best against: Deep Ones, Shoggoths, Star Spawn, Bosses
- Worse against: Hollow-Eyed, Reef Walkers (use normal ammo)

**Playstyle:** Strategic choice - save for tough enemies, or risk running out.

## Weapon Progression Path

**Starting (Tier 1):** All 3 weapons free  
**Early Unlock (800-1,200 scrip):** Upgrade primary OR secondary  
**Mid-Game (2,000-3,000 scrip):** Get automatic weapon  
**Late-Game (5,000+ scrip):** Elephant Gun OR save for ability upgrades  
**Endgame:** Blessed Rounds (one-time unlock)

**Recommended Unlock Order:**

1. Winchester 1897 or M1911 (800-1,000 scrip) - immediate improvement
2. Lee-Enfield (1,200 scrip) - if playing Lamplighter/Occultist
3. Thompson SMG (2,000 scrip) - major DPS upgrade
4. BAR (3,000 scrip) - highest DPS, but ammo-hungry
5. Elephant Gun (5,000+ scrip + bosses) - boss killer

## Weapon Comparison by Role

### Best for Headshots (Precision)

1. M1903 Springfield - 170 damage
2. Lee-Enfield - 160 damage
3. BAR - 90 damage per shot (full-auto)

### Best Close-Range Burst

1. Double-Barrel Shotgun - 240 damage (both barrels)
2. Elephant Gun - 500 damage (both barrels)
3. Winchester 1897 - 180 damage per shot

### Best Sustained DPS

1. BAR - 375 DPS (full-auto, hard to control)
2. Thompson - 257 DPS (full-auto, easier control)
3. M1911 - 152 DPS (semi-auto)

### Best Ammo Efficiency

1. Lee-Enfield - 10 rounds, good damage
2. Thompson - 30 rounds, spray friendly
3. .38 Revolver - Low damage but reliable

### Best Boss Damage (Single Magazine)

1. Elephant Gun - 500 damage (2 headshots)
2. BAR - 1,800 damage (20 headshots, if you land them all)
3. M1903 Springfield - 850 damage (5 headshots)

## Class Weapon Recommendations

### Bulwark (Tank)

**Primary:** Winchester 1897 → Thompson SMG  
**Secondary:** .38 Revolver → M1911  
**Why:** Close-range dominance, matches aggressive tank playstyle

### Lamplighter (Scout)

**Primary:** M1903 Springfield → Lee-Enfield  
**Secondary:** .38 Revolver → M1911  
**Why:** Long-range precision, scout from safety

### Harpooner (Aggressive)

**Primary:** Double-Barrel → Winchester 1897 → Thompson  
**Secondary:** .38 Revolver → M1911  
**Why:** Close-range after pulls, burst damage, shotgun → melee

### Occultist (Support)

**Primary:** M1903 Springfield → Lee-Enfield  
**Secondary:** .38 Revolver → M1911  
**Why:** Balanced range, support positioning from mid-range

## Ammo Scarcity Notes

**Starting Ammo (per weapon):**

- Rifles: 15-30 rounds total
- Shotguns: 6-15 shells total
- Pistols: 12-14 rounds total
- SMG: 90 rounds total
- BAR: 60 rounds total

**Ammo Sources:**

- World pickups (2 magazines)
- Kill drops (5-10% chance)
- Careful shooting (don't waste)

**Running Out:**

- Switch to secondary
- Use melee (50 damage base, 75 for Harpooner)
- Rely on team
- Extract early

## Recoil Patterns

**Vertical Only:**

- M1903 Springfield
- Lee-Enfield
- Double-Barrel Shotgun
- Winchester 1897

**Vertical + Horizontal:**

- Thompson SMG (climbs up-right)
- BAR (climbs up-right, heavier)
- .38 Revolver (slight right drift)
- M1911 (slight right drift)

**Extreme Kickback:**

- Elephant Gun (screen shake + knockback)

**Tip:** Fire in short bursts with automatic weapons to control recoil.

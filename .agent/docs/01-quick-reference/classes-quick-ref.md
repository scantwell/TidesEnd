## THE BULWARK (Tank/Anchor)

### Passive: Steadfast
- **Max HP:** 130 (vs. 100 base)
- **Damage Resistance:** +15% when below 50% HP
- **Knockback Resistance:** -50% (harder to knock back)

### Active Ability 1: Anchor Point
- **Cooldown:** 60 seconds
- **Cast Time:** 0.5 seconds (throwable)
- **Range:** 15 meters (throw distance)
- **Zone Radius:** 10 meters
- **Duration:** 20 seconds
- **Effects:**
  - Breach Saturation does NOT increase inside zone
  - Enemies move 30% slower inside zone
  - Allies cannot be knocked back inside zone

### Active Ability 2: Bastion Shield
- **Cooldown:** 90 seconds
- **Deploy Time:** 3 seconds (vulnerable while deploying)
- **Shield HP:** 500
- **Duration:** 30 seconds or until destroyed
- **Coverage:** Front 120° arc
- **Effects:**
  - Blocks all projectiles and melee from front
  - Allies can shoot over/around shield
  - Enemies cannot pass through

### Recommended Loadout
- **Primary:** Winchester 1897 or Double-Barrel Shotgun
- **Secondary:** Thompson SMG (if unlocked) or .38 Revolver
- **Playstyle:** Push to objectives, plant defensive positions, tank boss aggro

## THE LAMPLIGHTER (Scout/Intel)

### Passive: Pathfinder
- **Movement Speed:** +20%
- **Fog Vision:** +30% see distance through fog
- **Footstep Volume:** -40% (quieter, less likely to alert enemies)

### Active Ability 1: Flare Shot
- **Cooldown:** 45 seconds
- **Cast Time:** Instant (projectile)
- **Range:** 40 meters (arc trajectory)
- **Illumination Radius:** 20 meters
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Effects:**
  - Reveals enemy outlines through fog/walls
  - Enemies glow red while in radius
  - Teammates see enemy health bars

### Active Ability 2: Pathfinder's Mark
- **Cooldown:** 60 seconds
- **Cast Time:** Instant (ground target)
- **Range:** 20 meters (placement distance)
- **Zone Radius:** 15 meters
- **Duration:** 20 seconds
- **Effects:**
  - All teammates move +40% faster inside zone
  - Sprint stamina drain -50%
  - Jump height +20%
  - Reload speed +15%

### Recommended Loadout
- **Primary:** M1903 Springfield or Lee-Enfield (rifles for range)
- **Secondary:** M1911 Pistol or .38 Revolver
- **Playstyle:** Scout ahead, light dangerous areas, reposition constantly, mark priority targets

## THE HARPOONER (Aggressive Control)

### Passive: Sea Legs
- **Water Movement:** +15% speed in water
- **Reload Mobility:** Can reload while moving (others must stop)
- **Melee Damage:** +50% (75 damage vs. 50 base)

### Active Ability 1: Harpoon Tether
- **Cooldown:** 60 seconds
- **Cast Time:** Instant (skillshot projectile)
- **Range:** 20 meters
- **Projectile Speed:** 40 m/s (must lead targets)
- **Tether Duration:** 8 seconds
- **Tether HP:** 200 (can be destroyed by shooting it)
- **Effects:**
  - On hit: Enemy pulled 5 meters toward Harpooner
  - Enemy leashed to 10-meter radius from tether point
  - Enemy deals -20% damage while tethered

### Active Ability 2: Whaler's Frenzy
- **Cooldown:** 90 seconds
- **Cast Time:** Instant (self-buff)
- **Duration:** 10 seconds
- **Effects:**
  - Melee damage +40% (total: 105 damage with passive)
  - Melee attack speed +30%
  - Damage resistance +25%
  - Movement speed +10%

### Recommended Loadout
- **Primary:** Winchester 1897 or Double-Barrel Shotgun (close-range after pull)
- **Secondary:** M1911 Pistol (mid-range poke)
- **Playstyle:** Pull priority targets, aggressive positioning, melee when swarmed

---

## THE OCCULTIST (Support/Healer)

### Passive: Warded
- **Saturation Resistance:** Breach Saturation increases 25% slower for you
- **Healing Capacity:** Can carry 3 healing items (vs. 2 base)
- **Healing Bonus:** Healing items restore +25% more HP when you use them on others

### Active Ability 1: Sanctuary Circle
- **Cooldown:** 75 seconds
- **Cast Time:** 3 seconds (vulnerable while channeling)
- **Zone Radius:** 8 meters
- **Duration:** 25 seconds
- **Effects:**
  - Teammates regenerate 3% max HP per second (75% total if stay full duration)
  - Breach Saturation increase rate -50% inside circle
  - Damage resistance +10% inside circle

### Active Ability 2: Purge Corruption
- **Cooldown:** 90 seconds
- **Cast Time:** 5 seconds (vulnerable while channeling, cannot move)
- **Effect Radius:** 15 meters
- **Effects:**
  - Reduces Breach Saturation by 20% (global effect)
  - All teammates within radius gain 50 HP temporary shield (lasts 30 seconds)
  - Minor enemy stun (1 second disorient) in radius

### Recommended Loadout
- **Primary:** M1903 Springfield or Lee-Enfield (balanced rifle)
- **Secondary:** M1911 Pistol or .38 Revolver
- **Playstyle:** Stay near teammates, position circles strategically, manage saturation, shield before danger

## Team Composition Examples

### Balanced Comp (Recommended for New Players)
- **Bulwark** - Tanks and anchors
- **Lamplighter** - Scouts and lights
- **Harpooner** - Controls priority targets
- **Occultist** - Heals and manages saturation

### Defensive Comp (Extraction Focus)
- **Bulwark** - Plants shield + anchor at extraction
- **Bulwark** - WAIT, NO DUPLICATES ALLOWED
- Use: Bulwark, Occultist, Lamplighter (+ one DPS)

### Aggressive Comp (Speed Runs)
- **Lamplighter** - Speed zones to rush objectives
- **Harpooner** - Pulls priority targets quickly
- **Occultist** - Shields for aggressive pushes
- **Bulwark** - Emergency tank if things go wrong

### Boss Comp (Cathedral Extraction)
- **Bulwark** - Tanks boss, plants anchor to slow
- **Harpooner** - Pulls boss or adds into kill zone
- **Occultist** - Heals Bulwark, shields team
- **Lamplighter** - Reveals boss weak points with flare

## Class Synergy Matrix

| Combo | Synergy | How It Works |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Bulwark + Occultist | ★★★★★ | Circle behind shield = healing fortress |
| Bulwark + Harpooner | ★★★★☆ | Pull enemy into anchor zone = trapped |
| Bulwark + Lamplighter | ★★★★☆ | Flare reveals enemies → shield faces threat |
| Lamplighter + Occultist | ★★★★☆ | Speed zone + circle = fast healing rotations |

**Best Overall Synergy:** Bulwark + Occultist (defensive fortress)  
**Best Aggressive Synergy:** Lamplighter + Harpooner (marked target elimination)

## Ability Progression (Unlocks)

All classes start with Tier 1 abilities (base versions).

**Tier 2 Upgrades:**
- Unlocked at Class Level 5
- Cost: 500 scrip per ability
- Example: Bulwark's Anchor radius increases from 10m to 15m

**Tier 3 Upgrades:**
- Unlocked at Class Level 10
- Cost: 1,500 scrip per ability
- Example: Bulwark's Anchor causes enemies to take +10% damage inside

**Full upgrade paths:** See [../03-technical-specs/class-specifications.md](../03-technical-specs/class-specifications.md)

## Class Leveling Speed

**XP Gained Per Successful Extraction:**
- Shallows (1×): 100 XP
- Lighthouse (2×): 200 XP
- Cathedral (3×): 300 XP

**XP Required to Level:**
- Level 1 → 2: 200 XP (2 Shallows runs)
- Level 2 → 3: 300 XP
- Level 3 → 4: 400 XP
- Level 4 → 5: 500 XP (Unlock Tier 2 upgrades)
- Level 5 → 10: 600 XP per level
- Level 10+: (Unlock Tier 3 upgrades) 1,000 XP per level

**Time to Max a Class:**
~30-40 successful runs (15-20 hours playtime)

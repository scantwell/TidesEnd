## TIER 1: Corrupted Locals (0-25% Saturation)

### The Hollow-Eyed (Melee Rusher)

**Visual:** Gaunt fishermen, blank stares, mechanical shambling movements

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | 100 |
| **Damage** | 15 per melee hit |
| **Attack Speed** | 1 hit per 2 seconds |
| **Movement** | 3 m/s (idle), 5 m/s (alert) |
| **Detection** | 20m sight, 10m sound |
| **Threat Level** | Low (dangerous in groups) |

**Behavior:**
- Patrol predictable routes when idle
- Alert on player sight/sound
- Path directly to player, attack in melee range
- Do NOT give up chase (until 15s out of sight)

**Counters:**
- Kite backward while shooting
- Use doorways to funnel
- Headshots (2-3 shots from rifle)

**Spawns:** Baseline enemy, present at all saturation levels

---

### Reef Walkers (Ranged Harasser)

**Visual:** Amphibious cultists, corroded brass diving helmets, algae-covered suits

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | 150 |
| **Damage** | 25 per harpoon shot |
| **Attack Speed** | 1 shot per 3 seconds |
| **Movement** | 4 m/s (water), 3 m/s (land) |
| **Detection** | 25m sight, 15m sound |
| **Threat Level** | Medium (forces cover) |

**Behavior:**
- Emerge from water (ambush spawns)
- Maintain 15m distance (back up if player closes)
- Fire harpoon, duck behind cover
- Retreat to water at 50% HP

**Counters:**
- Close distance quickly (shotguns)
- Use cover to approach
- Focus fire (3-4 rifle shots)

**Spawns:** Present at all saturation levels, increased frequency 25%+

## TIER 2: Deep Spawn (25-50% Saturation)

### Deep Ones (Pack Hunter Melee)

**Visual:** Frog-like humanoids, webbed hands, gills, bulging eyes, greenish skin

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | 120 (each) |
| **Damage** | 20 per claw swipe |
| **Attack Speed** | 1 hit per 1.5 seconds |
| **Movement** | 6 m/s (faster than players!) |
| **Detection** | 30m sight, 20m sound |
| **Threat Level** | High (in packs) |

**Behavior:**
- **ALWAYS spawn in packs** (3-5 individuals)
- Coordinate flanking (2 front, 1-2 sides)
- Leap from water (5m jump, knockback)
- Focus injured players (scent blood)

**Counters:**
- Bulwark Anchor Point (slows them 30%)
- Focus fire one at a time
- Watch flanks constantly
- Use choke points

**Spawns:** 25%+ saturation, spawn rate increases with saturation

---

### Tide Touched (Environmental Hazard)

**Visual:** Oversized mutated animals (seals, gulls, crabs), too many eyes/limbs

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | 80-200 (variable by size) |
| **Damage** | 10-30 (variable by type) |
| **Attack Speed** | Unpredictable |
| **Movement** | Variable (some fast, some slow) |
| **Detection** | 15m sight, 10m sound |
| **Threat Level** | Low-Medium (hazards) |

**Behavior:**
- Patrol specific areas (territorial)
- Some phase through walls (2s intangibility)
- Unpredictable pathing (not intelligent)
- Block passages with bulk

**Counters:**
- Avoid if possible (not worth ammo)
- Bait phase, shoot when solid
- Use environment to bypass

**Spawns:** 25%+ saturation, random spawn locations

## TIER 3: Dimensional Intruders (50-75% Saturation)

### Shoggoths (Mini) (Area Denial)

**Visual:** Bubbling black protoplasm, forming/reforming eyes and mouths, pseudopods

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | 300 (splits at 150 HP into 2× 150 HP) |
| **Damage** | 10 per second (contact DOT) |
| **Attack Speed** | Continuous (contact damage) |
| **Movement** | 2 m/s (slow ooze) |
| **Detection** | N/A (reacts to proximity) |
| **Threat Level** | High (area denial) |

**Behavior:**
- Ooze through cracks/vents (can enter anywhere)
- Leave corruption trail (5 damage/sec)
- **Split when 50% HP** (now 2 smaller Shoggoths)
- **Reform if both survive 10s** (merge back into one)

**Counters:**
- Kill both halves quickly after split
- Avoid corruption trails
- Use grenades (AOE damage)
- Lamplighter flare reveals safe paths

**Spawns:** 50%+ saturation, often guards objectives

---

### Dimensional Shamblers (Teleport Hunter)

**Visual:** Mostly invisible, reality distortion shimmer gives away position

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | 200 |
| **Damage** | 40 (ambush strike, high burst) |
| **Attack Speed** | 8-second cooldown between teleports |
| **Movement** | N/A (teleports) |
| **Detection** | Targets isolated/injured players |
| **Threat Level** | Very High (solo), Medium (grouped) |

**Behavior:**
- **Invisible until 2s before attack** (shimmer intensifies)
- Teleport near player (within 5m)
- Strike, teleport away
- Prefer isolated targets

**Counters:**
- Stay grouped (harder to isolate)
- Listen for teleport audio cue (whoosh sound)
- Turn and shoot shimmer (visible)
- Lamplighter flare reveals outline

**Spawns:** 50%+ saturation, hunts stragglers

## TIER 4: Elder Manifestations (75-100% Saturation)

### Star Spawn (Elite Commander)

**Visual:** Octopoid head, scaled body, bat wings (human-sized, not kaiju)

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | 500 |
| **Damage** | 35 per tentacle lash |
| **Special Attack** | Reality blast: 50 damage, 15m cone, 10s cooldown |
| **Attack Speed** | 1 hit per 2 seconds (tentacle) |
| **Movement** | 7 m/s (flies, ignores obstacles) |
| **Threat Level** | Critical (buffs others) |

**Behavior:**
- **Commands nearby enemies** (+20% damage to all enemies within 20m)
- Casts reality distortion blasts (cone AOE)
- Flies to stay out of melee range
- **Retreats at 30% HP** (flees to summon more)

**Counters:**
- **PRIORITY TARGET** (kill first always)
- Focus fire immediately
- Harpooner pull to ground (prevents flying)
- Lamplighter flare tracks movement

**Spawns:** 75%+ saturation, critical threat

### The Formless (Invincible Hazard)

**Visual:** Shifting impossible geometry, hurts to look at, color distortions

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **HP** | **INVINCIBLE** (cannot be killed) |
| **Damage** | 10 saturation drain per second (proximity) |
| **Attack Speed** | Continuous (just being near) |
| **Movement** | 1 m/s (slow drift) |
| **Detection** | Patrols near rifts |
| **Threat Level** | Environmental Hazard |

**Behavior:**
- Cannot be damaged or killed (**AVOID ONLY**)
- Drains saturation rapidly in proximity (10m radius)
- Phases through walls
- Patrols near objectives/rifts

**Counters:**
- **Avoid entirely** (do not engage)
- Lamplighter speed zone to sprint past
- Occultist Purge to counter saturation drain
- Plan routes around it

**Spawns:** 75%+ saturation, guards high-value areas

## BOSS ENCOUNTERS (Extraction Point 3 - Drowned Cathedral)

### Boss #1: The Drowned Priest

**Visual:** Undead sorcerer, waterlogged robes, glowing runes, floating above flooded church floor

**HP:** 2,000 (3 phases: 2000→1400→800→0)

#### Phase 1 (100%-70% HP)
- Summons 8 Hollow-Eyed every 15 seconds
- Casts water spouts (3 random locations, 20 damage/sec)
- Teleports around arena every 30 seconds

#### Phase 2 (70%-40% HP)
- Summons 4 Deep Ones (pack)
- Water level rises (slows movement 30%)
- Tidal wave attack (75 damage, sweeps across arena, 8s telegraph)

#### Phase 3 (40%-0% HP)
- Summons 2 Star Spawn (elite commanders)
- All previous attacks active
- Enrage at 10% HP (attacks 50% faster)

**Strategy:**
- Phase 1: Kill adds, avoid spouts, chip boss
- Phase 2: Kite Deep Ones, dodge waves, burst during telegraphs
- Phase 3: Kill Star Spawn first, then burn boss

**Loot:** 1,000 scrip + Priest's Tome (2,000 scrip artifact)

---

### Boss #2: The Reef Leviathan

**Visual:** Whale-sized Deep One, covered in barnacles and coral, emerges from ocean

**HP:** 2,500 (no phases, sustained fight)

#### Mechanics
- **Tentacle Sweep:** 3 tentacles sweep floor (100 damage, knockback, 12s CD)
- **Whirlpool:** 8m radius, sucks players in (15 damage/sec, 20s duration)
- **Submersion:** Dives for 15s, leaps at random player (150 damage AOE)
- **Add Summon:** 6 Reef Walkers every 45 seconds

**Weak Points:** Barnacle clusters on back (2× damage, must position behind)

**Strategy:**
- Dodge tentacles (jump or speed zone)
- Escape whirlpools immediately
- Shield blocks tentacles
- Harpooner tethers tentacles to ground (prevents sweep 8s)
- Kill adds during submersion phase

**Loot:** 1,200 scrip + Leviathan Scale (2,500 scrip artifact)

---

### Boss #3: The Color (Rarest, 10% Spawn Chance)

**Visual:** Sentient light entity, impossible spectrum, drains color from environment

**HP:** 1,500 (mechanics-dependent kill)

#### Mechanics
- **Possession:** Takes control of 1 teammate for 10 seconds
- **Reality Distortion:** Inverts controls for 10 seconds (30s CD)
- **Color Drain:** Increases Breach Saturation 5%/second
- **Phase:** Invincible in light (must fight in darkness)

**Strategy:**
- Extinguish lights (shoot lamps, no Lamplighter flares)
- Shoot possessed teammate (50 damage breaks possession)
- Fight in darkness (Color vulnerable only then)
- Occultist Purge counters saturation drain
- **Time limit: 3 minutes** before saturation hits 100%

**Loot:** 2,000 scrip + Prismatic Shard (5,000 scrip, highest value)

## Enemy Spawn Distribution by Saturation

### 0-25% (Stable)
- **100% Tier 1** (Hollow-Eyed, Reef Walkers)
- **Spawn Rate:** 1 enemy per 15 seconds

### 25-50% (Fracturing)
- **70% Tier 1**, **30% Tier 2** (Deep Ones, Tide Touched)
- **Spawn Rate:** 1 enemy per 10 seconds
- **Special:** Ambush spawns enabled (spawn behind players)

### 50-75% (Breaching)
- **40% Tier 1**, **40% Tier 2**, **20% Tier 3** (Shoggoths, Shamblers)
- **Spawn Rate:** 1 enemy per 7 seconds
- **Special:** Director AI (spawns at chokepoints), Hunter enemies

### 75-100% (Collapse)
- **20% Tier 1**, **30% Tier 2**, **30% Tier 3**, **20% Tier 4** (Star Spawn, Formless)
- **Spawn Rate:** 1 enemy per 5 seconds
- **Special:** Continuous pressure, environmental hazards active, bosses can spawn

## Threat Priority List (Kill Order)

**Always Kill First:**
1. **Star Spawn** (buffs all enemies)
2. **Dimensional Shamblers** (burst damage to squishies)
3. **Reef Walkers** (ranged harassment)

**Kill When Convenient:**
4. **Deep Ones** (high threat in packs)
5. **Shoggoths** (area denial, but slow)
6. **Hollow-Eyed** (low threat, but alert others)

**Avoid/Ignore:**
7. **Tide Touched** (environmental, not worth ammo)
8. **The Formless** (invincible, just avoid)

## Enemy Weaknesses

**Weak to Headshots:**
- Hollow-Eyed (50% less HP than others, easy headshots)
- Reef Walkers (helmet can be shot off, then headshot vulnerable)

**Weak to AOE:**
- Shoggoths (splits damage both halves)
- Deep One packs (grenades hit multiple)

**Weak to Status Effects:**
- All enemies affected by Bulwark Anchor slow
- All enemies stunned by Occultist Purge (1 second)

**Resistant:**
- Star Spawn (flying, hard to hit)
- The Formless (invincible)
- Bosses (high HP pools)

## Enemy AI Behavior States

**All enemies follow this state machine:**

1. **Idle** → Patrol routes, no threat detected
2. **Alert** → Heard sound, investigating
3. **Combat** → Player in sight, engaging
4. **Flee** → Low HP, retreating (only Reef Walkers, Shamblers)

**Saturation Changes AI:**
- **25%+:** Ambush spawns (spawn in cleared areas)
- **50%+:** Coordinated attacks (flanking behavior)
- **75%+:** Aggressive pursuit (no safe zones)
